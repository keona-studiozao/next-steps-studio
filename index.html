Next Steps Studio

Not sure what comes next? Come and find out.

Next Steps Studio is a free, three-week programme for Roehampton students. Whether you're thinking about starting something of your own, or just trying to work out what your next career move looks like - this is a chance to try things out in the real world. You'll build and test a business idea with a team, use AI tools throughout, and pitch to people who can actually open doors. No experience needed, just bring your curiosity.

02 What Is Next Steps Studio

A chance to try entrepreneurship

You don't need a business idea. You don't need to know what you want to do yet. Next Steps Studio is three weeks to try something different - build something, test it, and see what clicks. Whatever comes next, you'll leave with more to work with.

01

Engage with real employers

Direct connections and live feedback from major industry partners throughout the programme.

02

Develop in-demand AI skills

You'll use AI tools to research, build and test ideas throughout the three weeks and leave knowing how to apply it.

03

Get coached and mentored

Studio Zao coaches in the room full-time, plus feedback from industry mentors throughout.

04

Build skills you can show

A prototype, a pitch deck, a digital badge via Navigatr, and real experience on your CV.

03 Programme Structure

What the three weeks look like

01Week One

Find Your Idea

  • Meet your team and get your partner challenge brief
  • Define the problem and who it affects
  • Talk to potential customers and gather early evidence
  • Use AI tools to research fast and build a first prototype
02Week Two

Test It in the Real World

  • Get your idea in front of real people and gather feedback
  • Improve your prototype based on what you hear
  • Work out your business model: who pays, how much, and why
  • Use AI to build out your marketing, data and back-office materials
03Week Three

Pitch It Live

  • Learn how to pitch with a masterclass from industry experts
  • Build your pitch deck and rehearse with mentors
  • Present your venture live to investors and employers
  • Leave with a personal action plan for what comes next
